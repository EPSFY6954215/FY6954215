Conference paper: The effect of Synthetic fuels on the automotive industry

Abstract

  This project provides an overview of synthetic fuels focusing on: their production processes ,benefits of synthetic fuels, limitations 
  and potential strategies to make synthetic fuels viable for mainstream use.
  These subtopics will provide insight to determine whether synthetic fuel is sustainable for the future.

introduction

  The transportation industry is evolving at a significant pace to meet deadlines set under tightening international regulations.
  Synthetic fuels have emerged to be the carbon-neutral solution for internal combustion engines. 
  Currently, synthetic fuels are being utilized in motorsports such as the dakar rally, formula 1 and in the operation of historic vehicles.
  Currently the market of synthetic fuel is expected to surpass $115 billion by 2026, this shows a clear demand to achieve net-zero emissions for transportation. 
  Companies such as Audi, Porsche, BMW and business start-ups are all investing heavily into synthetic fuel production(Saxtons, 2025).
